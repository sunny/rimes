Rime
====

Small cli to find rhymes. These are found using two dictionaries:

- `dict.dat`: a dictionary of words and their phonetic equivalent
- `syl.dat`: a dictionary of syllables and their phonetic equivalent

Usage:

  $ rime.rb cho
  artichaut (arti/o)
  poncho (pôt/o)
  réchaud (ré/o)
  bouchot (bu/o)
  manchot (mâ/o)
